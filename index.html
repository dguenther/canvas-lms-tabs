Canvas Tab Generator

  1. Name each tab below with a short, descriptive title. Titles over 20 characters may not fit all screen resolutions and are not allowed. You do not have to use all 5 tabs. Any tabs that you do not use will not appear on the page.
  2. Create each of your tabs as separate content pages in Canvas. When you are finished, click HTML Editor, copy your page, and paste the content in the corresponding tab below. Repeat for each tab/page.
  3. Click the 'generate' button below, copy the new HTML from the results section at the bottom of the page, create a new content page in Canvas, click on HTML Editor and poste in this HTML. You can edit the resulting content page, but if the HTML gets messed up, you will have to repeat this process.
